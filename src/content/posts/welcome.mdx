Building in public has several benefits:

- **Accountability**: Committing publicly helps stay motivated
- **Community**: Connecting with others on similar journeys
- **Learning**: Teaching others helps solidify understanding
- **Documentation**: Creating a record of the journey

- **R2 Storage**: Storing posts and assets
